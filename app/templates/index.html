🎤 Voice Command Cheat Sheet

Just say these commands and watch the magic happen!

Getting Started

"Hey Kitchen" Activate hands-free mode
"Help" Show this help menu

Recipe Search

"Find pasta recipes" Search for recipes
"Show me chicken video" Search YouTube videos
"Recipes with chicken and rice" Search by ingredients
"Play video 2" Open video detail page

Profile & Navigation

Click Profile button View stats, XP & achievements
Click any recipe result Open detailed recipe page
Click any video result Open video detail page

Timers

"Set a timer for 15 minutes" Create a timer
"Set pasta timer for 10 minutes" Named timer
"List timers" Show all active timers
"Delete timer 1" Remove a timer

Conversions

"Convert 2 cups to tablespoons" Unit conversion
"How many ml in 1 cup" Quick conversion

Substitutions

"Substitute for eggs" Find alternatives
"Replace butter in baking" Context-aware substitution

General

"What time is it?" Get current time
"What's today's date?" Get current date

💡 Pro Tips to Master Your Kitchen Assistant

Level up your cooking game with these insider tricks!

1

Use Natural Language

Don't memorize commands! Just talk naturally like you're asking a friend.

✅ "How do I make pasta?" ✅ "Show me pasta recipes" ✅ "Find me something with chicken"
2

Name Your Timers

Cooking multiple dishes? Name your timers so you don't get confused!

✅ "Set pasta timer for 10 minutes" ✅ "Set cake timer for 30 minutes"
3

Explore Video Detail Pages

Click on any YouTube video result to see a beautiful detail page with ingredients, steps, and notes!

✅ "Show me pasta video" ✅ Click any video card to open details ✅ Save videos to favorites & add notes
4

Track Your Progress

Visit your Profile to see XP, achievements, and cooking stats! Unlock achievements by trying new cuisines and cooking regularly.

✅ Earn XP for each recipe you cook ✅ Track your cooking streak ✅ View cuisine distribution charts
5

Explore Recipe Details

Click on any recipe search result to get a complete detail page with ingredients, steps, and nutrition info!

✅ Step-by-step cooking instructions ✅ Complete ingredient measurements ✅ Print or share recipes easily
6

Check Conversation History

Forgot a recipe you found yesterday? Click the button to review!

7

Hands-Free Mode

Enable hands-free and just say "Hey Kitchen" - perfect when your hands are messy from cooking!

8

Ask for Context

The AI understands context! Mention what you're cooking for better substitution suggestions.

✅ "Substitute oil in tandoori chicken" 🌟 Better than just "substitute oil"
9

Download Video Recipes

On video detail pages, click "Download Recipe" to save ingredients and steps as a text file!

10

Multiple Searches

You can search for recipes, watch videos, and set timers all in the same conversation!

11

Adjust Settings

Click to customize voice speed, volume, and more!
